Knitting Circle

Open Source Knitting Circle and (Not) Terrorist Society - a semi-regular gathering of open source technology professionals

View on GitHub

What?

Get together in a big conference room, hack on intersting stuff while interacting with other FOSS geeks in (shudder) meatspace, happy hour beers afterword.

Who?

Various folk from the local FOSS community who work for Canonical, Google, Mozilla, etc.

When?

Whenever we get around to planning one. Generally quarterly, sometimes more often.

Where?

Wherever we can find a room and bandwidth. OSU, Mozilla, and OPB have all hosted past knitting circle events.

Why?

'Cuz occupying the same vicinity in meatspace is fun! And there's beer.
